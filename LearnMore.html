Special Needs

Sepcial Needs

Programs designed to joyfully and fully include everyone in an integrated setting, with special attention paid to sensory, learning, and neuro-divergent needs, and programs designed for exclusively special needs audiences and settings. Andrea has experience in both formal educational and social settings with special needs adults and kids. She pays attention to content and performance from the start of program creation to all the moments of interaction, so that everyone is in the story.

Jewish Storytelling from a Jewish Storyteller

Jewish storytelling from a Jewish Storyteller

Andrea tells original and classic stories that span the depth and breadth of Jewish culture and religion.

No matter what your knowledge, faith community, or background, Andrea brings not only the lively spirit of Jewish storytelling but the compelling values within the stories.

- Jewish Holiday programs: Purim, Passover, Lag B’Omer, Shavuot, Rosh Hashanah, Yom Kippur, Shabbat and Rosh Chodesh.

- Original material and traditional tales from the Torah and midrash, and from rich and varied folk, rabbinic and mystical traditions of Jewish communities around the world and through the ages.

- Jewish stories for libraries, churches, and community groups.
