About Andrea

“Storytelling is gathering pieces of ourselves back to one piece.”
- Rosalie Zerrudo, Hilway Art Products, Philippines

I love a good troll story. I love going deep into the forest with witches and wolves. I love making new magic from the old.

There is no such thing as too cold for the beach.

Based in Boston MA, and virtually available worldwide

My sister has developmental disabilities and she and I have been storytelling with each other since she was first learning to speak. I have the skills to create inclusive experiences that meet all listeners where they are, allowing them to be their full selves.

An arts specialist, writer, teacher, community volunteer, and mom of five (plus two cats, and a crested gecko)

The stories we listen to matter, the way the people we listen to matter. I seek out and create diverse stories, so the “one” in the room can shout out, “That’s me!”

“With Andrea’s sophistication level, it's a good thing she’s on the side of good”
- Bruce Marcus, The Story Space
